How it feels when receiving a gift?

Gifts are a great source of happiness and smile in our lives. Be it any occasion or celebration, the tradition of giving and receiving gifts not only brings a smile on the face of the receiver but also makes the giver feel happy and excited.

How it feels when giving a gift?

The act of giving a gift has the power to strengthen bonds and foster deeper connections. Both the giver and the recipient experience a sense of joy, happiness, and satisfaction. The exchange of gifts creates a mutual sense of reciprocity and fosters a sense of belonging.
